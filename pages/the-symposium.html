7th International Symposium on Chemosynthesis-Based Ecosystems (CBE7)

CBE is the premier meeting of researchers, specialists, explorers, managers, policy makers and students to exchange ideas and share knowledge about advances in chemosynthesis-based ecosystems. We feel happy and honored to invite you for the 7th International Symposium on Chemosynthesis-Based Ecosystems (CBE7), which will be held in Sao Paulo city, Brazil between 14-18 August 2023 . For the first time, the symposium will take place in South America.

We are looking forward to offering you and spending with you an inspiring meeting with productive discussions, highlighting the new discoveries of CBEs, under the scope of the UN Ocean Decade.

See you soon in Brazil!

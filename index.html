What is CBE?

CBE is the premier meeting of researchers, specialists, explorers, managers, policy makers and students to exchange ideas and share knowledge about advances in chemosynthesis-based ecosystems. We feel happy and honored to invite you for the 7th International Symposium on Chemosynthesis-Based Ecosystems (CBE7), which will be held in São Paulo city, Brazil between 14-18 August 2023 . For the first time, the symposium will take place in South America.
